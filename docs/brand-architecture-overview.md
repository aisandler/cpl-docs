**HERO:** Residential real estate broker (primary) OR homebuyer (secondary)

**PROBLEM:**
- External: Need reliable legal representation for property transaction
- Internal: Anxiety about deal falling through, fear of attorney unresponsiveness
- Philosophical: Real estate transactions shouldn't be complicated by difficult attorneys

**GUIDE:** CPL Law Firm
- **Empathy:** "We know every closing matters to you and your clients"
- **Authority:** 50+ years combined partner experience, all-price-point expertise, 85-90% referral-driven

**PLAN:**
1. Easy access to partners (not just associates)
2. Proactive communication at every deal stage
3. Broker-as-partner approach (you look good, we succeed)

**CALL TO ACTION:**
- Direct: "Let's discuss your transaction"

**SUCCESS:** Smooth closings, strengthened broker-client relationships, confident buyers and sellers

**FAILURE AVOIDED:** Deal delays, surprise issues at closing, broker embarrassment with clients
