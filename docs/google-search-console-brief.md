## What Is Google Search Console?

**Google Search Console** is Google's free tool that shows you:
- What search terms people use to find your site
- Which pages appear in search results
- How many people click on your search listings
- Technical SEO issues preventing Google from indexing your site
- Performance trends over time

**Think of it as:** The measurement dashboard for SEO - like Google Analytics but specifically for search performance.
